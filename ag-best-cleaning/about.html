Who we are

AG Best Cleaning Services was started by Anna (Aninha) in Abington, Massachusetts. What began as a small residential cleaning operation has grown into a team serving over 300 clients across 40+ cities in Greater Boston.

We work Monday through Saturday, 8am to 6pm. Our team keeps consistent schedules, communicates clearly before each visit, and handles every job with the same attention to detail — whether it's a one-bedroom apartment or a commercial office space.

We are a women-owned business. That matters to us, and it shows in how we run things: straightforward communication, fair pricing, and a team that treats your space with care.

How we work

Four things that define how we show up for every client.

On-time, every time

We confirm appointments in advance and arrive when we say we will. Clients tell us this is one of the things they value most about working with us.

Clear communication

We text before visits, respond to messages quickly, and let you know if anything changes. No guessing about when we're coming or what we'll do.

Thorough work

We don't rush. Each job gets the time it needs. Clients who switch to us from other services consistently notice the difference in detail.

Consistent team

You'll see the same faces each visit. We believe familiarity builds trust, and a consistent team produces more consistent results.

Secure and respectful

Clients tell us they feel confident leaving us in their home. We treat every space with care and discretion, and we take that responsibility seriously.

Fair pricing

We quote honestly and don't add surprise charges. Several clients have noted that our pricing is reasonable for the quality of work delivered.
