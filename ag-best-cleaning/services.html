Cleaning Services

Residential and commercial cleaning across Greater Boston. No pricing packages — contact us for a quote based on your space.

What we offer

Each service is handled by the same team, with the same standards. We work Monday through Saturday, 8am to 6pm.

🧹 Deep Cleaning

A comprehensive clean that covers every surface, corner, and fixture in your home or apartment. Recommended for first-time clients, seasonal resets, or spaces that haven't been professionally cleaned in a while.

  • All rooms, floors, and surfaces
  • Kitchen appliances (inside and out)
  • Bathrooms — fixtures, tiles, grout
  • Baseboards, window sills, ceiling fans
  • Inside cabinets and closets (on request)

🏢 Commercial Cleaning

Office spaces, retail locations, medical facilities, and warehouses. We work around your operating hours and keep your workspace clean without disrupting daily operations.

  • Office spaces and conference rooms
  • Retail and storefront cleaning
  • Medical and clinical facilities
  • Warehouses and common areas
  • Flexible scheduling — before or after hours

🏠 Residential Cleaning

Regular maintenance cleaning for homes. We keep the same team for each visit so you're not introducing new people to your space every time. Weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly scheduling available.

  • Living areas, bedrooms, and hallways
  • Kitchen and bathrooms
  • Vacuuming and mopping all floors
  • Dusting surfaces and fixtures
  • Pet hair removal (dogs and cats)

🚗 Move In / Move Out Cleaning

Full cleaning before or after a move. We handle the details so you can focus on the transition itself — including appliances, cabinets, and all fixtures. Suitable for both landlords and tenants.

  • Full apartment or house clean
  • Inside refrigerator and oven
  • All cabinets and storage areas
  • Bathrooms deep cleaned
  • Walls and baseboards wiped down

🔨 Post-Construction Cleaning

Dust, debris, and residue removal after renovation or construction work. We restore the space to a clean, move-in ready condition. Suitable for residential and commercial properties.

  • Construction dust removal (all surfaces)
  • Window and glass cleaning
  • Floor cleaning and polishing
  • Fixture and hardware cleaning
  • Final walkthrough inspection

✨ First-Time Cleaning

An initial deep clean for new clients. We assess the space, discuss your priorities, and set a baseline before regular service begins. This visit helps us understand what your space needs on an ongoing basis.

  • Full walkthrough and assessment
  • Thorough clean of all areas
  • Discussion of recurring service options
  • No commitment required after first visit
  • Available for homes and offices
