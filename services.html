What we offer

Our Services

Cleaning services for residential and commercial clients in Abington and Greater Boston

01

Deep Cleaning

A comprehensive clean that covers every area of your home or office, including surfaces that routine cleaning skips. Suitable for first-time clients, seasonal cleans, or any time you need a full reset.

  • Kitchen: appliances inside and out, counters, sink, and fixtures
  • Bathrooms: scrubbed, disinfected, and descaled
  • Baseboards, window sills, door frames, and vents
  • Inside cabinets and closets
  • Heavy pet hair removal
  • Laundry services available by the hour

02

Commercial Cleaning

Scheduled cleaning for offices, retail locations, and other commercial facilities. We work around your hours to keep your space clean without disrupting operations.

  • Office desks, surfaces, and equipment
  • Restroom sanitation and restocking
  • Vacuuming, mopping, and floor care
  • Kitchen and break room cleaning
  • Common area and lobby maintenance
  • Recurring weekly or bi-weekly scheduling

03

Move In / Move Out Cleaning

Prepare a property for new occupants or leave your previous space in the condition it should be. We handle the full property so you can focus on the move itself.

  • Complete room-by-room clean
  • Appliances, fixtures, and all surfaces
  • Cabinets and closets emptied and cleaned
  • Suitable for landlords, tenants, and property managers

04

Post-Construction Cleaning

After renovation or construction work, dust and debris settle everywhere. We remove it thoroughly so the space is ready to use.

  • Construction dust and debris removal
  • Window cleaning inside and out
  • Hard surface and floor cleaning
  • Fixture and hardware polish

05

First-Time Cleaning

For new clients, the first visit is a full deep clean to establish a baseline. This allows us to understand your space and set up a maintenance schedule that fits your needs.

  • Full property assessment and deep clean
  • Establishes a cleaning baseline for recurring visits
  • Flexible scheduling to suit your availability

06

Residential Cleaning

Regular home cleaning on a schedule that works for you. We keep your home clean between deep cleaning visits.

  • Weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly options
  • Consistent team assigned to your home
  • Flexible rescheduling when needed
